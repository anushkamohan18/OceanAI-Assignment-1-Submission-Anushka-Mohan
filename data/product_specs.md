# Product Specifications

## Discount Codes
- **SAVE15**: Applies a 15% discount to the total cart value (excluding shipping).
- **FREESHIP**: (Not implemented yet, but reserved) Free shipping.

## Shipping Methods
- **Standard**: Free. Delivery in 5-7 business days.
- **Express**: Costs $10.00. Delivery in 1-2 business days.

## Cart Logic
- Base prices:
  - Wireless Headphones: $99.00
  - Phone Case: $19.00
- Total calculation: (Sum of items * Discount Multiplier) + Shipping Cost.

## Payment Methods
- Credit Card
- PayPal
